# Privacy Policy for FreeCoffee

**Last Updated:** January 23, 2026

**Effective Date:** January 23, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to FreeCoffee ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the FreeCoffee mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you use the FreeCoffee app, we collect the following information:

- **Phone Number**: Your 10-digit phone number for campaign notification delivery
- **Platform Preference**: Your device platform (Android or Apple)
- **Push Notification Token**: A unique device token to send push notifications (automatically generated by your device)

### Information Automatically Collected

- **Campaign Interaction Data**: When you view campaigns in the app, we may log basic usage analytics
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting
- **Network Status**: Whether you're online or offline (stored locally only, never transmitted)

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:
- Your name, email address, or physical address
- Payment or credit card information
- Location data (GPS or otherwise)
- Contacts or phone book data
- Photos, videos, or media files
- Browsing history or other apps on your device
- Any personally identifiable information beyond your phone number

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

1. **Campaign Notifications**: To send you push notifications when new Capital One coffee campaigns become available
2. **Service Delivery**: To deliver campaign information to your subscribed phone number
3. **Widget Updates**: To display real-time campaign counts in the home screen widget
4. **Troubleshooting**: To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance
5. **Aggregate Analytics**: To understand overall app usage patterns (no individual tracking)

## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, we process your information based on:

- **Consent**: You explicitly consent when subscribing your phone number
- **Legitimate Interest**: To provide the notification service you requested

## How We Share Your Information

### We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

### Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

1. **Supabase (Database Hosting)**
   - Purpose: Store phone numbers and campaign data
   - Data Shared: Phone number, platform preference, push token
   - Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy
   - Location: United States
   - Security: SOC 2 Type II certified, encryption at rest and in transit

2. **Expo Push Notification Service**
   - Purpose: Deliver push notifications
   - Data Shared: Push notification token (device identifier)
   - Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy
   - Location: United States
   - Note: Expo does not have access to notification content

3. **Capital One API**
   - Purpose: Validate coffee campaign links
   - Data Shared: Campaign IDs only (no personal information)
   - Note: Publicly accessible API, no authentication required

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as:
- To comply with a court order or subpoena
- To protect our legal rights
- To prevent fraud or abuse
- To respond to government requests

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption in Transit**: All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS
- **Encryption at Rest**: Database encryption via Supabase
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to backend systems
- **Regular Updates**: Security patches applied promptly
- **No Plain Text Passwords**: No authentication system (phone number only)

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

- **Active Subscriptions**: We retain your phone number as long as you remain subscribed
- **Unsubscribed Users**: Phone numbers are permanently deleted within 30 days of unsubscribing
- **Campaign Logs**: Message delivery logs retained for 90 days for troubleshooting
- **Cached Data**: Local device cache cleared when you uninstall the app

## Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

### Right to Access
You can view your subscribed phone number in the app's Settings screen.

### Right to Rectification
To update your phone number, unsubscribe and re-subscribe with the correct number.

### Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)
Unsubscribe in the app Settings to permanently delete your data within 30 days.

### Right to Data Portability
Contact us at privacy@freecoffee.app to request a copy of your data (phone number and platform).

### Right to Object
You may object to processing by unsubscribing from the service.

### Right to Withdraw Consent
You can withdraw consent at any time by unsubscribing in the app.

## Children's Privacy

FreeCoffee is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Union). We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information should contact us at privacy@freecoffee.app.

## International Users

### GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

- You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

### CCPA Compliance (California Users)
